Generating Images Part by Part with Composite Generative Adversarial Networks

TLDR: the generative adversarial network(GAN) was successful in generating high quality samples of natural images. They propose a model called composite generative adversarial network,that reveals the complex structure of images with multiple generators in which each generator generates some part of the image. GAN simultaneously trains two models: a generator G that tries to generate real images, and a discriminator D that classifies between the real images that came from the training data and the fake images that came from G.
