Topic Augmented Neural Response Generation with a Joint Attention Mechanism

TLDR:

Background: sequence-to-sequencemodel and attention mechanism. In sequence to sequence, given a source sequence \(X=\{x_1,x_2,...,x_T\}\), the model maximizes the generation probability of \(Y\) conditioned on \(X\): \(p(y_1,\cdot,y_{T'}|x_1,\cdot,x_T)\). The objective function of Seq2seq can be written as : \(p(y_1,...,y_{T'}|x_1,...,x_T)=\prod_{t=1}^{T'}p(y_t|c,y_1,...,y_{t-1})\). The encoder RNN calculates the context vector \(c\) by \(h_t=f(x_t,h_{t-1});c=h_T\).
