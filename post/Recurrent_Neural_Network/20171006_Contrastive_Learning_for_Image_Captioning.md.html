Contrastive Learning for Image Captioning

They propose a new learning method, Contrastive Learning (CL), forimage captioning. Specifically, via two constraints formulated on top of a reference model, the proposed method can encourage distinctiveness, while maintaining theoverall quality of the generated captions.

In Contrastive leanring, they learn a target captioning model \(p_m(:;\theta)\) with parameter \(\theta\) by constrainting its behaviors relative to a reference model \(p_n(:;\phi)\) with parameter \(\phi\).

For any pair \((c,I)\), the target model and the inference model will respectively give their estimated conditional probabilities \(p_m(c|I, \theta)\) and \(p_n(c|I, \phi)\). We wish taht \(p_m(c_t|I_t, \theta)\) is greater than \(p_n(c_t|I_t,\phi)\) for any positive pair, and vice versa for any negative pair.
