Visually grounded learning of keyword prediction from untranscribed speech

They use an image-to-words multi-label visual classifier to tag images with soft textual labels, and train a neural network to map from speech to soft targets.

Model overview

  • Training image \(\mathbf{I}\)
  • A spoken caption of frames \(\mathbf{X}=x_1, \cdots, x_T\), encoded with MFCC
  • Target network : \(\mathbf{Y}_{det\ vis}=f(\mathbf{X})\)
