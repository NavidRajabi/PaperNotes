Optimization loss

For triaining they are only using two losses: 1. GAN loss: \(\mathcal{L}_{cGAN}(G,D)\) 2. L1 loss (enforce correctness at low frequencies): \(\mathcal{L}_{L1}(G)\) 3. The final objective is : \(G^{\ast}=\arg\min_G\max_D\mathcal{L}_{cGAN}(G,D)+\lambda \mathcal{L1}(G)\)
