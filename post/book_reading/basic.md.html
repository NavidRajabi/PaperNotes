多媒体基本知识

图像

数字视频是spatio-temporal采样的视频组合。

常见的图像空间有：RGB, HSV, HLS, \(YC_rC_b\). RGB是我们常见的图像，但是RGB不是一个有效表示的组合，HVS对luminance(亮度)比颜色更敏感。所以\(YC_rC_b\)有优势，归纳如下： 1. \(C_r,C_b\)可以用比较低的分辨率表示，而\(Y\)用全分辨率表示。

常见数字图像格式： 1. GIF: Graphics Interchange Format 2. JPEG: Joint Photographic Experts group 3. PNG: Portable Network Graphics

Graphics

Graphics is a vector image -- Mathematical descriptions of an image. Graphics primitives: lines, curves, circles. Attributes: thickness, gray-scale, color.

videos

  1. Analog video
  • composite video : chrominance and luminance signals are mixed in a single carrier wave.
  • component video : each component is carried in its own line
  • s-video : a compromise between composite video and component video. It uses two lines, one for luminance and another for a mix of chrominance signals.
  1. Digital video
  • MPEG : all developed by motion picture experts group. open format distributed by ISO. Joint video team still working on MPEG-4;
  • DV : each manufacturer has their own particular version of the format
  • RealMedia : Streaming technology developed by RealNetworks for transmitting live video over the Internet.
